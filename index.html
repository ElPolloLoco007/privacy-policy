Privatlívspolitikkur

Dagfest: 6. apríl 2026

Varp er eitt ókeypis forrit til at stroyma føroyskt útvarp og sjónvarp. Hetta lýsir hvussu appurin viðger upplýsingar.

Hvat vit savna

Varp savnar so lítið sum møguligt:

  • IP-adressa — til at kanna um brúkarin er í Føroyum ella uttanlands, so rættur streymur verður valdur. Hetta gerst gjøgnum eitt API-kall til ipapi.co. Vit goyma ikki IP-adressuna.
  • Stillingar — tínar val (t.d. sjónvarpsrásir, tøkur, frættikeldur) verða goymd lokalt á tíni eindu (AsyncStorage). Ongar stillingar verða sendar til okkara ambætara.

Hvat vit ikki savna

  • Ongin kontogerð ella innritan
  • Ongin greining (analytics) ella rakningarforrit (tracking)
  • Ongar smáfunnar (cookies) á netútgávuni, undantikið tey sum stroymarar krevja
  • Ongir persónligar upplýsingar verða goymdar ella deildar

Triðja-parts tænastur

  • ipapi.co — IP-staðsetingaruppslag (bert land, einki meira)
  • Kringvarp Føroya / Midlar — stroymar lúðinga- og sjónvarpsefni
  • Google Cast — Chromecast-útsendingarvirksemi, stýrt av Google

Børn

Varp er ikki serstakliga ætlað børnum og savnar onga upplýsing frá børnum.

Broytingar

Vit kunna dagføra hendan politikk. Broytingar verða birtar her.

Privacy Policy (English)

Effective: April 6, 2026

Varp is a free app for streaming Faroese radio and TV. This describes how the app handles information.

What we collect

  • IP address — used once per session via ipapi.co to determine if you are in the Faroe Islands (for selecting the correct stream URL). We do not store your IP address.
  • Preferences — your settings (theme, channels, news sources) are stored locally on your device only. Nothing is sent to our servers.

What we do not collect

  • No account creation or login
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No cookies on the web version, except those required by stream players
  • No personal data is stored or shared

Third-party services

  • ipapi.co — IP geolocation lookup (country only)
  • Kringvarp Føroya / Midlar — audio and video stream providers
  • Google Cast — Chromecast functionality, governed by Google's privacy policy

Children

Varp is not specifically directed at children and does not collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here.
